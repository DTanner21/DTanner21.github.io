Alex loved finding things—rocks, bugs, even old coins sometimes. Today felt like a lucky day.
ALEX: (Excited) "Come on, there’s gotta be something cool under here!"
With a determined scoop of the shovel, something unexpected happened. *Clink!* The shovel hit something hard. Alex froze.
ALEX: (Blinking in surprise) "Huh? What was that?" They tossed the shovel aside and started digging with their hands. Slowly, something small and metallic began to appear.
ALEX: (Whispers) "Whoa… a key? But what does it open?"
Alex stood up, scanning the yard. Their heart beat faster. Somewhere out there was a lock waiting to be opened…
