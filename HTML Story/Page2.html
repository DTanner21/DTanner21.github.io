ALEX: (Thinking) "Where would a secret door be...?"
Alex walks to the garden shed and peers behind it. Nothing but old tools.
ALEX: (Sighing) "No secret door here."
As they turn away, something catches their eye—a small piece of paper poking out from the grass.
ALEX: (Whispering) "What’s this?"
They bend down and carefully pick it up, unfolding it. The paper feels old, and the writing is faint.
ALEX: (Reading aloud) "Find the hidden door. The key will guide you to what’s inside."
Alex’s pulse quickens. This was it—the next clue!
ALEX: (Excited) "A hidden door!"
They look back at the key in their hand, now certain there’s something waiting to be unlocked.
